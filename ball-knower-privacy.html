Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Ball Knower ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application ("App").

This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use. By using Ball Knower, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Username/Display Name: If you choose to appear on our leaderboard, you may provide a display name.
  • Feedback: If you contact us for support, we collect the information you provide.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Identifier: A unique identifier for your device, used to save your game progress and preferences locally.
  • Gameplay Data: Information about how you play the game, including scores, streaks, and game modes played.
  • Daily Game Count: We track the number of games you play each day to enforce free tier limits.
  • App Usage: General usage statistics such as session duration and feature usage.

Purchase Information

When you make in-app purchases, we collect:

  • Transaction ID: A unique identifier for your purchase from Apple's App Store.
  • Product ID: Which subscription or product you purchased.
  • Purchase Date: When the purchase was made.
  • Subscription Status: Whether your subscription is active, expired, or cancelled.
  • Expiration Date: For subscriptions, when your current period ends.
Important: We do NOT collect or store your credit card number, billing address, or other payment details. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple through the App Store.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Save your game progress and high scores
  • Display your scores on the global leaderboard (if you choose)
  • Process and manage your premium subscription
  • Enforce daily game limits for free users
  • Verify purchase receipts and prevent fraud
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the game experience
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and support requests
  • Send you technical notices and updates

Legal Requirements

Public Leaderboard

If you submit a high score, your display name and score may be visible to other users on the global leaderboard. You can choose not to submit scores or use an anonymous name.

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Free Tier

Ball Knower offers a free tier that allows you to play up to 100 games per day. We track your daily game count to enforce this limit.

Premium Subscription

We offer the following premium options:

  • Monthly Subscription ($0.99/month): Unlimited games, auto-renews monthly
  • Lifetime Access ($2.99): Unlimited games forever, one-time purchase

Subscription Terms

  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase
  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period
  • You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your App Store account settings
  • No refunds are provided for partial subscription periods

Receipt Validation

When you make a purchase, we verify your receipt with Apple's servers to confirm the transaction and activate your premium status. This process does not expose your payment information to us.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS
  • Data at rest is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We use secure cloud infrastructure (AWS) with compliance certifications
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple's secure systems

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Our legal basis for processing your data includes: performance of a contract (providing the App), legitimate interests (improving our services), and consent (where applicable).
